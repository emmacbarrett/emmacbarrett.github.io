Hello!

I'm a first-year PhD student and the V.O. Key Fellow at Harvard University's Department of Government. I study American Political Behavior, with a focus on political geography. At Harvard, I am affiliated with the Center for American Political Studies.

Prior to coming to Harvard, I earned my B.S. in Political Science and International Affairs from Florida State University.
